The Archipelago Story

Start with the Why

In October 2024, Queen Arun Sandhya wrote the constitution for the Archipelago. A document stating "Property belongs to the legal owner of the property".


Any organisation could identify with the Archipelago, which meant that it would follow that constitution. A number of entities joined, and this enabled trading, for those members, and partners, and the initiatives came to be. The initiatives are goals, to resolve conflict, and other challenges that are international.


There were a number of initiatives early on that enabled some key conflicts to be resolved, and provide stability in those regions. These include Eire (former Ireland), Myanmar, and Tarousha Santosh (Israel, Jordan, and Palestine). As issues were resolved one by one, markets opened up it was decided, that the Archipelago would have a trading aspect to it, to aid the areas.


The Archipelago is a group of communities that align by their commonalities to achieve initiatives, through business and other means.


There may be no conflict as such, within the group. There may only be a challenge, like in the case of Kalyteros, the Experiences initiative to reduce modern slavery.

The Archipelago organisation consists of crisis response, marketplace, legal(international and domestic), inspectorate, and Counsel.


Our communities are bound together by commonalities, and we stand with solidarity. We can trade. We can sing. We can dance. Together.
Ethos and Pathos. Kalyteros. Better together.
Ideals and skills. Stronger Together.

Diverse and Trusted.




Who is involved?

Aruna Sandhya Koya founded the Archipelago ideals and brought together the Archipelago Counsel to resolve international issues.

The Archipelago Counsel consists of world leaders, and brings us together when we can’t see past differences in opinion or culture. We stand with respect, and we talk. Teams include crisis response, dispute resolution with international trade lawyers, and guardians.

Alongside the Archipelago Community, companies participate in initiatives to provide a collaborative and lucrative marketplace.




What is involved?

Archipelago Counsel — meeting, talking, planning, and responding to crises. You are not alone. Your company is not alone. Your country is not alone. Your community is not alone.

Archipelago Constitution — respect for boundaries. Every owner has a right to their property. A legal foundation.

Archipelago Community Ltd — trading together through our platform and initiatives so our people can prosper.




How does it work?

Communities — we come together around a commonality.

Initiatives — we work together toward shared goals.

Incentives — we benefit from trading together.
