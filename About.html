About the Network

Why we exist and how we work together.

Start with the Why

Communities stand with solidarity, and we all feel we have someone to share with. We can trade. We can sing. We can dance. Ethos and Pathos. Kalyteros. Better together. Ideals and skills. Diverse and Trusted. Stronger Together.

Archipelago Community is a network of independent groups connected through shared values, cooperation, and mutual support.

Our network is a collective of independent groups working together through shared values, open communication, and mutual support.




Who is involved?

Aruna Sandhya Koya, founded the Archipelago ideals, and brought together the Archipelago Counsel to resolve international issues.

The archipelago counsel, brings us together, when we can’t see past our differences in opinion, our cultural differences. We stand with respect for each other, and talk. Teams include crisis response, dispute resolution with international trade lawyers, guardians.

As well as the Archipelago Community there are companies that take part in the initiatives to provide a lucrative marketplace.




What is involved?
