Archipelago Community

A global community of independent consortia working together to address shared challenges, advance collaboration, and build trusted market infrastructure.

About the Community

The Archipelago Community is a collective of purpose-driven consortia. Each consortium operates independently, with its own governance and members, while benefiting from shared principles, collaboration frameworks, and operational support.

Consortia

Each consortium focuses on a specific domain, enabling members to collaborate on non-competitive initiatives with clarity, trust, and impact.

Banking Consortium

Collaboration among banks to address shared challenges in compliance, fraud, payments, and infrastructure modernization.

Consortium of Exchanges

A neutral forum for exchanges to collaborate on market infrastructure, resilience, cybersecurity, and regulatory alignment.

Payments & Settlement Consortium

Focused on interoperability, settlement efficiency, and modernization of payments and post-trade processes.

Digital Assets Consortium

Supporting responsible innovation in digital assets through shared standards, infrastructure, and risk management.

Data & Standards Consortium

Developing common data models, taxonomies, and standards to improve interoperability across markets.

Operational Resilience Consortium

Strengthening systemic resilience through shared frameworks, testing, and best practices.

Join the Archipelago Community

Organizations that believe collaboration is a strategic advantage are invited to explore participation in one or more consortia.
