Start with the Why

The Archipelago Community site was created to solve world challenges, similar to the UN's Sustainable Development Goals


The key to The Archipelago is that communities, get together and Archipelago It!

What does the Archipelago offer?

The Archipelago focuses on delivering the intiatives focused on solving world challenges.
Each initiative has certain themes, and the solutions fall into those themes.
Communities stand with solidarity to solve the challenges step by step, and with lucrative returns.
