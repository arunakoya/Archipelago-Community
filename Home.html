Start with the Why

The Archipelago Community site was created to solve world challenges, similar to the UN's Sustainable Development Goals

The key to The Archipelago is that communities, get together and Archipelago It!

Want to conserve energy resources, Archipelago It!
Come along to the Anektos - Climate Initiative site, and search amongst the solutions offered by companies.

Want to contribute towards reduction in CO2, Archipelago It!
Come along to the Anektos - Climate Initiative site, and offer a solution.

Want to find new Educational resources, Archipelago It!
Come along to the Biblios - Education Initiative site, and search amongst the solutions offered by companies.

The Archipelago focuses on delivering the intiatives focused on solving world challenges. Each initiative has certain themes, and the solutions fall into those themes. Communities stand with solidarity to solve the challenges step by step, and with lucrative returns.
